## Presentation Equations

### Dipole

#### Equation 1

$$\mu = - \frac{\partial E}{\partial F}$$

#### Equation 2

$$\frac{\partial E}{\partial F} = \langle \psi | \frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial F} | \psi \rangle + \frac{\partial E_{nuc}}{\partial F}$$

#### Equation 3

$$\hat{H}(F) = \hat{H}_0 + F_x \hat{x} + F_y \hat{y} + F_z \hat{z}$$

#### Equation 4

$$\frac{\partial \hat{H}}{\partial F_x} = \hat{x}$$

#### Equation 5

$$\frac{\partial E}{\partial F_x} = \langle \psi | \hat{x} | \psi \rangle + \frac{\partial E_{nuc}}{\partial F_x}$$

#### Equation 6

$$\mu_x = -\frac{\partial E}{\partial F_x} = -\left(\sum_{\mu \nu} P_{\mu \nu} D^x_{\mu \nu} + \sum_{A} Z_A \mathbf{R_{A,x}}\right)$$

#### Equation 7

$$D^x_{\mu \nu} = \langle \chi_{\mu} | x | \chi_{\nu} \rangle$$

### $D_{\mu \nu}$

#### Equation 1

$$D^x_{\mu \nu} = \langle \chi_{\mu} | x | \chi_{\nu} \rangle$$

#### Equation 2

$$S_{\mu \nu} = \langle \chi_{\mu} | \chi_{\nu} \rangle$$

#### Equation 3

$$D^x_{\mu \nu} = \sum_{k} \sum_{l} d_{k \mu} d_{l \nu} N_{k \mu} N_{l \nu} D_x^{kl}$$

#### Equation 4/5

$$g_A = (x - X_A)^{l_A} (y - Y_A)^{m_A} (z - Z_A)^{n_A} e^{\alpha |r - R_A|^2}$$

$$g_B = (x - X_B)^{l_B} (y - Y_B)^{m_B} (z - Z_B)^{n_B} e^{\beta |r - R_B|^2}$$

#### Equation 6

$$D_x^{AB} = \int g_A(r) \cdot x \cdot g_B(r) dr$$

#### Equation 7

$$D_x^{AB} = [S_x^{{AB}} (l_{A} + 1, l_{B}) + X_{A} S_x^{{AB}}(l_{A}, l_{B})] S_y^{AB} S_z^{AB}$$
